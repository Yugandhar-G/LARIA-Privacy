# Delete Your LARIA Account

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

If you wish to delete your LARIA account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

## How to Delete Your Account

### Option 1: Delete Account from Within the App

1. Open the LARIA app on your device
2. Go to **Settings** (accessible from the menu)
3. Navigate to **Account Settings** or **Profile**
4. Tap **Delete Account** or **Remove Account**
5. Confirm the deletion by entering your password or confirming your identity
6. Your account and data will be permanently deleted

- **Immediate Deletion**: Account data, chat history, and personal information are deleted immediately upon account deletion
- **Backup Retention**: Encrypted backup data may be retained for up to **90 days** for disaster recovery purposes, after which it is permanently deleted
- **Legal Retention**: Any data required by law may be retained as required by applicable regulations

## Third-Party Services

Please note that deleting your LARIA account does not automatically delete your data from third-party services:

- **Google Account**: If you signed in with Google, you may need to revoke LARIA's access in your Google Account settings
- **Firebase**: Account deletion removes your data from Firebase Authentication, but you may need to separately manage your Google account

## After Account Deletion

- You will no longer be able to sign in to LARIA
- All your data will be permanently removed (subject to retention periods above)
- You can create a new account at any time, but previous data cannot be recovered
